// SIGNAL ORIGIN

Mantis Vex is a French producer working in darksynth, horrorsynth and cyberpunk — cinematic, aggressive electronic music shot through with cosmic horror, eldritch dread and the slow collapse of meaning.

From the slasherwave menace of It, That Craves to the glitched dystopia of Glitchpunk, every release is its own self-contained nightmare. No sunsets. No nostalgia. Just the static and what's behind it.
